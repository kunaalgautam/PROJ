Project Proposal

My website will discuss recent MMA news, show current rankings, and give people unfamiliar with the sport a chance to understand it. I want them to be able to appreciate the sport despite the negative reputation that it has.

The objective of this website is to create new MMA fans who will appreciate the sport and begin to follow it. Another objective is to clarify any misunderstandings that people have about the sport.

Since this is a web page that is open to everyone, my target audience would include both fans of MMA and those who are not.

I will include a homepage that has an introduction that explains the sport to those unfamiliar with the sport. I will also have an about me page that explains the creator of the page and gives a brief background about me. I will have a standings page that shows current rankings of fighters in different weight classes in the UFC (I am choosing the UFC as it is the most popular MMA promotion company). I will include a news page that displays current news about the sport including future matchups, call-outs, important interviews etc. There will be a page that also explains the history of the sport so viewers can see how successful the sports expansion has been over the past 20 years. I will also add a blog page where I discuss fights, announcements, news and give my opinions on them.
