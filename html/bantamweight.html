Possible Bantamweight Contender?

UFC Vegas 44 ended with a bang as Jose Aldo dominated Rob Font for five whole rounds, giving him the unanimous decision. This poses the question whether or not Jose Aldo has redeemed himself for another title shot. His last opportunity was on July 11, 2020 at UFC 251 where he lost by TKO to Petr “No Mercy” Yan. Aldo utilized his leg kicks and precise but powerful striking to stun and hurt Font on numerous occasions throughout the fight. Although, Font returned with some big shots of his own, it was quite clear who was leading and dominating the entire fight. Font had been the favorite leading up to the fight, but I guess the underdog took the steal. I believe Aldo should fight another top contender before being given the opportunity to fight for the belt. Some possible contenders include TJ Dillashaw or Cory Sandhagen. Whether he can beat Yan again is a question that we must see until Aldo faces either of these two.
