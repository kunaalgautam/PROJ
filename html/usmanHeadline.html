Who can Dethrone Usman?

Kamaru “The Nigerian Nightmare” Usman is truly a spectacle of the welterweight division. He is currently on a 19 fight winning streak and only has 1 loss. He is unbeaten in the UFC and has knocked out top welterweight contenders including Colby Covington, Jorge Masvidal, and Gilbert Burns. Covington was expected to give the hardest fight to Usman, yet Usman made easy work of him during their second bout for a dominant unanimous decision win. Usman has always been known for his spectacular wrestling, however he has recently been using a secret tool that has given him notable recognition which is the jab. His front hand holds so much power and he was able to drop Covington and Burns with it on numerous occasions. His jab keeps his opponents at distance, giving Usman the opportunity to counter or throw other devastating strikes that the opponent cannot see. When it looks like theres no one there to dethrone the welterweight king, who is there to look for as a possible contender? Well, look no further than Khamzat “Borz” Chimaev, the Swedish-Chechen nightmare for Usman. Khamzat recently participated in a freestyle-wrestling contest with UFC middleweight Jack “the Joker” Hermannson where he completely dominated the bout for seven straight rounds. Hermannson is known for his wrestling and ground-game, and to see Chimaev (who was recently brought into the UFC) derail him like that, brings about the question as to whether he can truly dethrone Usman. Khamzat not only has phenomenal wrestling in which he choked out Li Jingliang recently in the first round, but his striking is not to be overlooked. He was able to knockout Gerald Meerschaert in a mere 19 seconds. Khamzat Chimaev could seriously pose a threat to the welterweight and middleweight divisions.
