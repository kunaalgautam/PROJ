Teixeira given key to Danbury

In a touching and deserved moment, new UFC light heavyweight champion Glover Teixeira attended a parade thrown in his honor at his hometown of Danbury, Connecticut. The champion recently got his belt after submitting Jan Blachowicz in round 2 at UFC 267 in Abu Dhabi. Despite being older than most of the UFC roster at a bright age of 42 years, he was still able to conquer and beat the best of the best in the light heavyweight division. Many believe that Teixeira deserved to get the belt before he retires, and it made many people proud that he was able to achieve his dream of becoming champion. After the parade, Teixeira was awarded the key to the city of Danbury, in which he owns a gym. Rock on Glover! We are with you!
