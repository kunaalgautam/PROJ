UFC 269 Poirier vs Oliviera

Our main event is underway as both Dustin Poirier and Charles Oliviera made weight today on Friday. UFC 269 will take place in Las Vegas and is expected to be one of the highest revenue generating PPV events of the year. The two lightweight contenders are expected to put on a blast as Brazil’s Charles “Do Bronx” Oliviera (31-8) makes his first title defense against Louisiana’s Dustin “The Diamond” Poirier (28-6). Oliviera is expected to try and take this fight to the ground, given his phenomenal jiu-jitsu. However, it is important to understand that his striking is quite precise, as he was able to knockout “Iron” Michael Chandler in his last bout that won him the championship belt. Poirier, when asked about his odds as a challenger for the belt, explained how it is important to not doubt Oliviera’s striking and ground-game. Poirier himself is quite talented in his ground-game but expects to keep the fight standing to ensure his boxing skills reign and gives him the chance to knockout Oliviera. As of 12/10/2021, Poirier stands as the -160 favorite, and Oliviera is the +140 underdog.
